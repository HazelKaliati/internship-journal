Reflection & Personal Growth

One of my most challenging experiences was dealing with a rude provider. I had to stay professional and file an incident report, which taught me how to handle difficult situations in a workplace environment.

At the beginning of my internship, I also struggled with feeling like I didn’t fully fit in due to personality differences. However, over time I became more comfortable with my team and started asking questions more confidently.

My supervisor and team were very supportive and helped me grow both technically and personally throughout the internship.

Overall, this experience has been very meaningful and has helped me gain confidence in a real-world IT environment.
