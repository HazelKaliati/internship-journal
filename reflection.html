Reflection & Personal Growth

Challenges I Faced

One of the most challenging moments during my internship was dealing with a difficult interaction with a provider. I had to remain professional in the moment and later file an incident report. That experience really taught me how to stay calm and professional even when situations are uncomfortable.

Another challenge I faced at the beginning was adjusting socially. I sometimes felt like I didn’t fully fit in because of personality differences, and it took me time to get comfortable in that environment.

Growth & Confidence

As time went on, I became more confident asking questions and engaging with my team. I started to understand that learning is part of the process, and I didn’t have to know everything immediately.

Support From My Supervisor & Team

My supervisor and team played a huge role in my development. They were patient, supportive, and always willing to guide me when I was unsure. Their encouragement helped me grow not only technically but also personally in how I approach work environments.

Overall Reflection

Overall, this internship has been a very meaningful experience. It helped me gain confidence, build technical skills, and understand what working in a real healthcare IT environment looks like. I’m leaving this experience with more clarity, growth, and appreciation for the field.
