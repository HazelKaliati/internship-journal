My Internship Experience

When I first started my internship at Faith Health, I didn’t know what to expect. Our first project was re-imaging computers. This involved using a drive, accessing the boot menu, and deploying systems configured for the organization.

At first, it was intimidating because it was my first time doing it. However, we followed step-by-step documentation in OneNote. Over time, we became confident and no longer relied heavily on instructions.

As time went on, we were given more responsibilities. We worked with Active Directory for password resets and account unlocks. Password resets became one of the most common support tickets we handled.

We also worked in field support, setting up workspaces and moving computers between departments and buildings due to office relocations.
